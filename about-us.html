Who We Are

Infotel Myanmar is the official partner of PA Infotel Co., Ltd, a leading regional provider of hospitality technology solutions across Vietnam, Cambodia, Thailand, Singapore, and Myanmar. We bring global expertise and tailor it for the Myanmar hospitality industry.
